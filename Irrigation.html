    • Why is palm oil important?

    The oil palm tree, which can only be grown in tropical climates, yields premium oil that is largely used for cooking in developing countries. Additionally, it is utilized in detergents, cosmetics, biodiesel, and culinary goods. Palm oil is a very productive crop. Compared to other vegetable oils, it gives a significantly higher output at a cheaper cost of production.
    Palm oil production and demand are both rising quickly on a global scale.
